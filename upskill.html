About Us

Upskill is one the one many initiatives under IEEE Region 10. This event aims to start professional development activities at the section level for young professionals besides providing a platform to participants to enhance their soft skills for a successful career.

The IEEE Delhi Section, one of the 16 regions in India that comes under the Region 10 (Asia-Pacific) of IEEE, has also been a part of the initiative and is organizing the program Upskill at the Delhi Section level.

Upskill 2021 Delhi section has a lot in store for its participants. Intriguing events such as webinars and talks on various topics such as leadership, Sustainable development, engineering in society, management for engineers. Upskill will also hold workshops to help participants to develop or enhance their soft skills. The key and most fun activity of the Upskill will be the Networking sessions. The participants will interact and connect with other participants from various regions and thus expand their network. Upskill's Interactive sessions with experts from different fields will help you gain knowledge and open new horizons to explore. Have time to hear the perspective of experts on the topic in the panel discussion and take part with your input during the event. Upskill as an event is an experience that can help you change and enhance your career by gaining insights from people currently working in your field and learning about new methods and skills to elevate your professional image. With the right amount of effort in the right direction, you can move mountains. Upskill will provide you with the first step!

Why Join YP ?

IEEE YP is the group of IEEE members who have graduated with their first professional degree in the last 15 years. It is a community where you will find people from recent graduates from universities to professionals who are experienced in the field and entrepreneurs.

Reasons to join YP are many, following are some:

IEEE Young professional is a hub where you can network with professionals at every level of experience be it a fresher or a high operating executive in any field.

You can connect at a global level with peers and members globally and expand your network.

You will elevate your professional image by being a member here. Your career can get the boost it needs with networking and developing skills.

You will also be able to connect and interact with your peers locally at events, gatherings, and seminars organized by IEEE YP.

IEEE YP can also provide you with an opportunity to give back to the community by volunteering at various events organized by YP and contribute your bit towards the greater good. Volunteering has its benefits!

IEEE Young Professional is highly diverse in terms of people and in what it offers. It has a network of over 120,000 IEEE members.
